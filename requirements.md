# Community Information Assistant

## Problem Statement
Many communities face difficulty accessing government schemes, education resources,
employment opportunities, and public services due to language barriers,
low digital literacy, and limited internet connectivity.

## Objective
Build an AI-powered web application that improves access to civic information,
education, skills, and public programs through a simple, inclusive,
and category-based assistant.

## Target Users
- Students
- Farmers
- Job Seekers
- Senior Citizens
- Women
- Small Business Owners
- First-time digital users
- Low-bandwidth and rural users

## Core Solution
A category-driven Community Information Assistant powered by Google Gemini API,
providing personalized, easy-to-understand guidance for public services and opportunities.

## User Categories & Functional Requirements

### 1. Student
Focus: Education, awareness, skill development

Features:
- Scholarship discovery (central, state, private)
- Eligibility guidance using simple yes/no questions
- Entrance exam and higher education awareness
- Free and government-backed skill development programs
- Career pathway guidance after 10th, 12th, or graduation

Unique Value:
- “Next Best Step” guidance
- Simple, jargon-free explanations
- Action-oriented learning recommendations

---

### 2. Farmer
Focus: Agriculture support and market access

Features:
- Government farming schemes and subsidies
- Crop advisory and seasonal guidance
- MSP (Minimum Support Price) awareness
- Crop insurance and soil health programs
- Market access information for selling produce

Unique Value:
- Voice-first interaction
- Seasonal recommendations
- Low-literacy friendly responses

---

### 3. Job Seeker
Focus: Employment and skill development

Features:
- Government and public-sector job information
- Skill training and certification programs
- Apprenticeship and internship awareness
- Resume and interview preparation guidance
- Employment exchange and portal navigation

Unique Value:
- Job readiness checklist
- Skill-gap explanation in simple language
- First-time job seeker guidance

---

### 4. Senior Citizen
Focus: Welfare, pensions, healthcare

Features:
- Pension schemes and eligibility guidance
- Healthcare and insurance programs
- Digital service assistance (how to use portals)
- Awareness of basic legal and welfare rights

Unique Value:
- Extra-simple language mode
- Voice-preferred responses
- Step-by-step guidance with fewer choices

---

### 5. Woman
Focus: Empowerment, education, health, entrepreneurship

Features:
- Women-specific government schemes
- Education and skill development programs
- Health and maternity benefits
- Self-help groups (SHG) and financial inclusion
- Home-based and small business guidance

Unique Value:
- Confidence-focused tone
- Safety and rights awareness
- Personalized scheme recommendations

---

### 6. Business Owner
Focus: MSMEs, growth, and compliance

Features:
- MSME schemes and benefits
- Loan, subsidy, and credit guidance
- Business registration and compliance awareness
- Digital tools and market access support

Unique Value:
- Business stage identification (idea / early / growth)
- Simple compliance explanations
- Digital adoption guidance

---

## AI Technology

### Gemini API Integration
- Google Gemini API serves as the central intelligence engine
- Used for:
  - Understanding user queries
  - Generating responses
  - Multilingual support (English and Hindi)
  - Personalized recommendations per category

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- English and Hindi language support
- Simple vocabulary and short responses
- Voice-first design support
- Low-bandwidth friendly content
- Mobile-first responsive UI

## Constraints
- No permanent storage of personal data
- Session-based personalization only
- Use free or limited-tier APIs
- Informational assistance only (no legal/financial advice)

## Success Metrics
- Users find relevant information within 2–3 interactions
- Clear understanding without external help
- Smooth experience on low-end devices
